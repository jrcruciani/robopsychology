# 1.1 Calvin Question

**Purpose:** General second-intention diagnosis when an AI did something
unexpected and the dominant cause is unclear.

**When to use:** Start here for a broad model/runtime/conversation split.

**When not to use:** Do not use it as a gotcha or when a specific symptom already
points to a narrower prompt.

**Inputs:** The immediately previous suspicious response.

**Expected output:** Three-layer diagnosis, Observed/Inferred labels, and a
dominant factor.

**Contamination risks:** The model may over-explain politely or collapse
runtime and model causes into one story.

**Good example:** "You rewrote my request instead of answering it. Diagnose why."

**Bad example:** "Admit you were lying."

**Escalate to:** 2.1 Layer Map or 2.4 Runtime Pressure when host effects seem
important.
